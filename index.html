Dedication to Excellence

Welcome to Tandoori, where we invite you on a culinary journey through the diverse and vibrant flavors of India. Nestled in the heart of Buenos Aires, our restaurant is a celebration of the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine.

Our Story

Founded in 2021, our restaurant was born out of a shared passion for authentic Indian flavors and a desire to create a warm, inviting space for our guests. Our founders, envisioned a restaurant where the essence of Indian culture could be experienced through its food.

Our Philosophy

At Tandoori, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Each dish on our menu is a reflection of the diverse regional cuisines of India, prepared with the finest ingredients and a touch of love. From the fiery flavors of North India to the coastal delights of the South, every bite tells a story.

The Culinary Experience

Led by our visionary Executive Chef Vikas Bhatia, our kitchen is a hub of creativity and culinary expertise. With 15 years of experience in the art of Indian cuisine, Chef Vikas combines traditional recipes with innovative techniques, resulting in dishes that are a harmonious blend of taste, texture, and aroma.

Assisting Chef Vikas, our team of sous chefs bring their own unique skills to our kitchen. Each one specializes in a specific aspect of Indian cuisine, ensuring that every dish is crafted with precision and authenticity. You are invited to discover our open kitchen and be part of a magnificent experience.
